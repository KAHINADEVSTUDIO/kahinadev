Branding

Universe

In the universe of the Romanum project, the history of the city of Rome is reflected. Each colour has been meticulously chosen to evoke a facet of this rich history.

* Black: evokes the depths of the catacombs where lie the darkest secrets of the city.
* White: symbolizes the bright light that bathes on the majestic Roman ruins, recalling the past greatness of the Empire.
* Brown: recalls the warm hues of old cobbled roads.
* Blue: it evokes the serenity of the calm waters of the Tiber, highlighting the link between the city and its iconic river.

Each of these colours contributes to creating an immersive atmosphere that captivates the very essence of Rome’s history.
