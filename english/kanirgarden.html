Introduction

Serenity and Balance: Welcome to KanirGarden

KanirGarden is a spa and wellness center project created in 2024. The mission is to provide an experience of relaxation and revitalization through a range of high quality services.

  • Year 2024
  • Services Development / design
  • Type E-commerce

Concept

KanirGarden’s concept is based on the idea of creating a wellness sanctuary, where guests/users can easily book sessions, buy quality spa products, and discover tips.

Graphic charter

Deep purple

Rich and deep color represents spirituality, meditation and transformation. It evokes inner wisdom and emotional balance.

Snow white

Evokes purity, clarity and tranquility. It creates a luminous and soothing canvas for our space, symbolizing inner peace and purity of mind.

Purple mauve

This soft and soothing purple is associated with creativity, intuition and healing. It encourages inspiration and deep reflection, creating an environment conducive to personal growth and spiritual fulfillment.

Purple lilac

Embodies relaxation, balance and softness. It diffuses a soothing and comforting energy.

Purple lavender

This delicate shade of purple evokes tranquility, healing and inner beauty. It creates a harmonious and peaceful atmosphere.
