Password: kanirgarden

The project of redesigning this old project, serves to adapt the online presence of a fictitious company of spa and wellness center.

The main objective is to move from a simple showcase site to an e-platformThe Internet is a comprehensive commerce platform, allowing customers to discover and purchase products online in a convenient and secure way.

Goal of the project

The redesign of the KanirGarden site aims to modernize the online presence and provide a superior user experience. The objectives of this project are not limited to a simple aesthetic update, but also to a series of strategic improvements.

E-commerce integration


Go from a showcase site to a complete e-commerce platform, allowing the management of orders, online payments and promotions.

Modernization of the brand image


Improve the siteâ€™s aesthetics and design to reflect a more modern and professional brand image.

User experience (UX)


Offer a more intuitive and fluid navigation, with better organization of content and products.

Optimization for mobile devices


Ensure an optimal user experience on all devices, including smartphones and tablets.

Improve the accessibility


Make the site accessible to a wider audience, including users with specific needs.

Before

The old logo had a simple, minimalist design with a functional approach, emphasizing legibility and simplicity but lacking creativity.

After

The new logo embodies a more artistic and detailed style with a mix of sophistication, which symbolizes a more refined and creative identity.

Redesign

Before

- Layout: Simple structure with a linear organization and basic presentation of products and information.
- Interactivity: Limitation of interactive elements, which can make the user experience static and unengaging.
- Adaptability: Design less optimized for mobile viewing, resulting in navigation problems on smaller screens.

After

- Layout: Use of a more sophisticated layout with separate and well-organized sections for products, testimonials, and company information.
- Interactivity: Introduction of animations, smooth transitions and interactive features such as zooming in on product images and carousels.
- Adaptability: Responsive site, offering a consistent and optimized browsing experience across all devices with an adaptive user interface.
